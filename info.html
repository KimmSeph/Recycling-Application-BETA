About EcoBin

EcoBin is an innovative waste management system designed to promote sustainable practices. Our mission is to reduce waste and make recycling easier for everyone. By tracking waste bin locations, we ensure that users can always find the nearest disposal unit, making waste management more accessible and efficient. Join us in making the world a cleaner place!
