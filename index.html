What is EcoBin?

EcoBin is a mobile-friendly system that helps you properly dispose of your waste using QR codes on bottles, bins, and packaging. No logins. No downloads. Just scan and throw responsibly.

Mission

To empower individuals and institutions to manage waste sustainably through easy-to-use, tech-based solutions.

Vision

A clean, eco-friendly environment supported by innovative and accessible waste disposal practices.

Implemented At

Batangas State University - Alangilan Campus
This system is proudly initiated to promote campus sustainability and responsible waste habits.
